Niko Bellic

Niko Bellic is the protagonist and player character in Grand Theft Auto IV. Niko is of Serbian descent, and previously fought in the Yugoslav Wars, possibly for Republika Srpska, although it is unclear which side he fought on. He is wooed to Liberty City by promises of living the American Dream from his cousin Roman Bellic, but when his plans go sour, he turns to the seedy underworld of Liberty City to build his way to the top.
