Claude

Claude is a criminal who traveled to Liberty City to engage in more criminal activity. In the opening stages of the game, he is betrayed by his then-girlfriend, Catalina. Now under arrest, he manages to escape with his new acquaintance 8-Ball while they're been transported to Portland Island. From then onwards, he is out for revenge on Catalina while also forming alliances with many factions from Liberty City's criminal underworld. Claude is the only protagonist in GTA history to not speak, just occasionally making sounds.
